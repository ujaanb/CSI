# Hypothyroidism

## What it is
Hypothyroidism (underactive thyroid) is a condition of inadequate thyroid hormone production, presenting with fatigue among many systemic features and, in its primary form, characterised biochemically by high TSH with low T3/T4 (see [Mina Shahnawaz – Tiredness](../sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md)).

## Properties
- **Type:** condition
- **Owner / Operator:** —
- **Status in our context:** a key pathological cause of fatigue in the Phase 1B CSI tiredness case.

## Description
### Biochemistry
Primary hypothyroidism gives **high TSH, low T3/T4** because the failing thyroid removes the negative feedback that normally suppresses TRH/TSH (see [HPT axis](../concepts/hpt-axis.md)).

### Causes
- Autoimmune thyroid disease, e.g. [Hashimoto's disease](./hashimotos-disease.md) (see [source](../sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md))
- Radioactive iodine treatment (see [source](../sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md))
- Antithyroid drugs (see [source](../sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md))
- Medicines such as [lithium](./lithium.md) and [amiodarone](./amiodarone.md) (see [source](../sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md))
- Excess iodine (e.g. some cough medicines; kelp/seaweed in excess) (see [source](../sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md))
- Pituitary malfunction (see [source](../sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md))
- Radiation for head and neck cancers (uncommon in the UK) (see [source](../sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md))
- Congenital hypothyroidism (see [source](../sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md))

### Symptoms
Fatigue/tiredness, cold intolerance, dry/coarse skin, dry/thinning hair, hoarse voice, constipation, muscle weakness/cramps/aches, carpal tunnel syndrome (pins and needles in fingers/hands), heavier and longer periods, fertility problems, low libido, weight gain, puffy face with bags under the eyes, slow speech/movement/thought, low mood or depression, memory problems, difficulty concentrating, bradycardia, slightly raised blood pressure, and raised cholesterol (see [source](../sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md)).
