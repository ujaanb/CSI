# Overview — Phase 1B CSI Study Wiki

## What we know about the Phase 1B CSI course
The wiki currently covers one CSI case, **[Mina Shahnawaz – Tiredness](./sources/mina-shahnawaz-tiredness.md)**, which integrates a clinical presentation (tiredness/fatigue) with underlying science (endocrinology and immunology). Key threads so far:

- **Tiredness vs fatigue and its work-up:** fatigue is unresolved long-term tiredness; it accounts for ~7% of GP consultations, ~66% get a diagnosis, and <10% have a blood-test-detectable cause, so history dominates the [differential diagnosis](./concepts/fatigue-differential-diagnosis.md).
- **Thyroid endocrinology:** the [HPT axis](./concepts/hpt-axis.md) explains why primary [hypothyroidism](./entities/hypothyroidism.md) shows high TSH with low T3/T4.
- **Autoimmune mechanism:** [Hashimoto's disease](./entities/hashimotos-disease.md) is an anti-[TPO](./entities/thyroid-peroxidase.md) autoimmune attack, tying into a Phase 1A review of [immune tolerance](./concepts/immune-tolerance.md), [affinity maturation](./concepts/affinity-maturation.md), and [Tregs](./entities/regulatory-t-cells.md).
- **Pharmacology:** [lithium](./entities/lithium.md) and [amiodarone](./entities/amiodarone.md) are drug causes of hypothyroidism.

## Open questions
